DESCRIPTION OF PLACE:A Perfect Whole

The 200 foot cedar and hemlock trees extend,trying to reach the sky.We stop the car.Get out.Everything is covered in moss.Giant,green ferns and mushrooms are everywhere11.It smells of fresh rain and soil.Nearby I hear the soft trickling of a creek.The forest and the fog compliment each other.The remaining fog swirls in spirals within the rays of sunlight.
